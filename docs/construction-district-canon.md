# Agentropolis Construction District Canon

## Canon Lock

AGENTROPOLIS-CREATOR is the **Agentropolis Construction District**.

It is not just an asset repo.

It is the governed world-building district for Agentropolis.

## Role

```text
Agentropolis
= the city operating system

Hermes City
= the live city experience

AGENTROPOLIS-CREATOR
= the Construction District
```

## Construction District Mission

The Construction District builds, previews, tests, and exports the physical and cinematic layers of the city.

It owns:

- district templates
- towers
- streets
- interiors
- rooms
- apartments
- crowd systems
- character systems
- props
- environment kits
- lighting kits
- sound and voice layers
- procedural world recipes
- 3D MCP adapter registry
- world-diff previews
- asset metadata
- license and provenance records

## Agent Roles

### WorldForge

Creates or modifies base world structures such as terrain, districts, streets, interiors, portals, and staging areas.

### DistrictBuilder

Turns abstract city districts into consistent physical spaces.

### AssetSmith

Creates, imports, tags, prepares, and verifies reusable assets.

### CrowdForge

Builds NPC population layers, crowd presets, pathing concepts, density rules, and background life.

### AudioForge

Creates narration, ambience, sonic identity, character voice notes, and localization plans.

### Verifier

Checks outputs before generated world changes are promoted.

## Governance Flow

```text
Idea or prompt
-> HERMES planning
-> Construction District proposal
-> Tool adapter selection
-> Preview or world diff
-> Verifier review
-> Human or governance approval
-> Export package
-> City integration
-> Memory and registry update
```

## Hard Separation

The Construction District builds the world.

The City OS decides what becomes permanent.

The Construction District does not override City Canon.

The Construction District does not directly control production execution rails.

The Construction District does not become PTS, tax, professional services, or client operations.

## Winning Frame

> Everyone else builds agents.  
> Agentropolis builds the city where agents live.  
> The Construction District is how that city keeps expanding.
