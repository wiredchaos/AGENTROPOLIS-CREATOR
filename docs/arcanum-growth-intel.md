# ARCANUM GROWTH INTEL

**System:** Agentropolis Creator / Hermes Stack  
**District:** CIVITAS GROWTH  
**Type:** Hermes Skill Pack  
**Tagline:** Spy. Score. Spin. Audit. Deploy.

## Canon Lock

ARCANUM GROWTH INTEL is not a Claude Code plugin. It is a Hermes-compatible Agentropolis skill pack for paid media intelligence and agentic growth workflows.

It converts competitor ad research, category mapping, ad copy generation, creative scoring, and live Meta account auditing into modular skills that can be used by Agentropolis Creator.


## Skills

### meta-ad-spy
Pull active competitor Meta ads and rank them by longevity. Longevity is treated as a signal of possible creative durability, not guaranteed performance.

### category-map
Compare 3 to 5 brands head-to-head and identify messaging white space, overused claims, underused angles, offer gaps, and attack lanes.

### creative-swarm
Generate 20 on-brand ad variations from a winning angle or category gap.

### creative-score
Grade ads 0 to 100 across hook strength, offer clarity, audience fit, differentiation, conversion intent, and compliance risk.

### account-audit
Audit a connected Meta ad account through official Meta MCP/API access and produce a prioritized fix list.

## Governance Rules

- No automatic ad spend.
- No automatic campaign launch.
- No client-side Meta tokens.
- No plaintext credential storage.
- Human review required before launch.
- Every recommendation should create an audit event.
- Live ad account data must be treated as sensitive client data.
